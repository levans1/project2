Student Interviews
What do Da Vinci Students have to say about Vans?

I Interviewd four Sophomores at Da Vinci and asked them what their favorite shoe brand is.
All of them said Vans.
The quotes inserted below are the reasons why they like Vans.

Me: "Whats your favorite shoe brand?"
Reese Gonzalez:"Vans"
Me: "Why?"
Reese:"Because when you go skating it's more comfortable. Vans are more for the comfort"

Me: "Whats your favorite shoe brand?"
Marc Cazarez: "Vans!"
Me: "Why?"
Marc:"They're comfortable and you can skate in them and you have grip.
There's many different colors to them, you can customize them"

Me:"What's your favorite shoe brand?"
Morgan Daniels: "Vans"
Me: "Why?"
Morgan:"Because they're so affordable and comfortable and they go with everything."

Me: "What's your favorite shoe brand?"
Morgan Newson: "Lately it's been Vans"
Me: "Why?"
Morgan N: "Because of the customs and they're not that expensive."
